Employee Non-Productive Activities Report

Calculation Formula

Formula Example Result
Non-Productive Index = (Non-Productive Hours ÷ Total Working Hours) × 100 (2 ÷ 9) × 100 22.22%
Productive Hours = Total Working Hours − Non-Productive Hours 9 − 2 7 Hours
Productivity Index = (Productive Hours ÷ Total Working Hours) × 100 (7 ÷ 9) × 100 77.78%

Example:

Working Hours = 9 Hours
Non-Productive Hours = 2 Hours

Non-Productive Index = (2 ÷ 9) × 100 = 22.22%
Productive Hours = 9 − 2 = 7 Hours
Productivity Index = (7 ÷ 9) × 100 = 77.78%
